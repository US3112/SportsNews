Messi to join MLS side Inter Miami after PSG exit

Lionel Messi will make a stunning move to MLS side Inter Miami after his departure from Paris Saint-Germain, the World Cup winner with Argentina announced Wednesday

Lionel Messi has announced he will join Inter Miami CF in Major League Soccer (MLS) after leaving Paris Saint-Germain (PSG). The deal is not yet finalized, but Messi expressed his desire to experience a different style of football and enjoy a calmer daily life in Miami. The move is expected to be a major boost for Inter Miami and the biggest signing in MLS history. Barcelona wished Messi luck and stated they had made an offer before his decision to join MLS. Messi's arrival is anticipated to revolutionize the game in the United States.
