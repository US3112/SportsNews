Jude Bellingham Joins Real Madrid For Next Six Seasons

Madrid have officially announced the signing of Jude Bellingham from Borussia Dortmund on Wednesday

Real Madrid has reportedly spent 100 million euros ($108 million) to sign talented young midfielder Jude Bellingham from Borussia Dortmund. Bellingham, who spent three years at Dortmund, made a significant impact with the club, attracting attention from top European teams. He made 132 appearances for Dortmund, scoring 24 goals and providing 25 assists. Dortmund confirmed that an agreement had been reached with Real Madrid, with the transfer fee amounting to 103 million euros, plus potential bonuses of up to 30 percent of that fee. Bellingham expressed gratitude to Dortmund and its fans in a farewell message, stating that he will never forget his time with the club. Dortmund's sporting director, Sebastian Kehl, also wished Bellingham the best for his future at Real Madrid.
